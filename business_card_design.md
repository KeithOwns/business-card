## Design Specifications

To ensure the physical cards look as good as the digital concept, here are the exact structural and stylistic guidelines you can provide to a printer (like Moo or VistaPrint):

### 1. Card Stock & Finish
*   **Paper Quality:** Ultra-thick (32pt or 38pt) to immediately communicate stability and premium service.
*   **Finish:** Matte or Soft-Touch finish. Avoid glossy (it looks cheap and easily smudges). 
*   **Special Touches (Optional but recommended):**
    *   **Spot UV:** Add a subtle glossy raised effect to the "AI+IT" logo on the matte background.
    *   **Painted Edges:** Electric Cyan edges to make the cards pop when stacked.

### 2. Color Palette
*   **Primary Background:** Deep Indigo / Dark Charcoal (`#1a1b26` or `#0f172a`)
*   **Text (Primary):** Off-White (`#f8fafc`) for maximum legibility against the dark background.
*   **Text (Secondary/Labels):** Slate Gray (`#94a3b8`) for titles and labels so they don't overpower the info.
*   **Accent (Logo/Dividers):** Electric Cyan (`#06b6d4` or `#0ea5e9`) to represent the "AI" and modern tech aspect.

### 3. Typography
Use a modern, geometric sans-serif font that feels both technical and approachable.
*   **Primary Font:** `Inter`, `Outfit`, or `Montserrat`.
*   **Name:** Bold, 12pt-14pt.
*   **Title/Role:** Regular or Medium, 8pt-9pt, in Slate Gray.
*   **Contact Info:** Regular, 9pt-10pt.

### 4. Anatomy of the Card

**Front (The Brand Statement):**
*   **Centered:** The "AI+IT Support" logo or stylized text.
*   **Minimalist:** Let the dark background and accent colors do the talking. No clutter.

**Back (The Contact Layer):**
*   **Left Aligned (or structured grid):**
    *   **Keith [Last Name]** (or just Keith)
    *   *AI Consultant*
*   **Contact Block:**
    *   `keith@aiit.support`
    *   `323-364-4408`
    *   `aiit.support`
*   **Location Marker:**
    *   *West Los Angeles, CA* (Establishes local trust immediately)
*   **QR Code (Highly Recommended):**
    *   Place a small QR code in the bottom right corner that links directly to your digital calendar or the `/contact` page on your website, making it frictionless to book an AI audit.
